    The Security Service of Ukraine (SBU) arrested Igor Kolomoisky. He is accused of illegally transferring over 500 million hryvnias abroad in 2013-2020. Kolomoisky is the president of the United Jewish Community of Ukraine, vice-president of the Football Federation of Ukraine, former head and member of the European Council of Jewish Communities, president of the European Jewish Union (EJU), as well as the founder of Privatbank, Ukraine’s largest bank with over 22 million clients. What is happening, what are the versions?

    Version 1: Democrats are avenging Kolomoisky for helping Republicans compromise Biden’s son. Previously, by conspiring against Poroshenko and promoting Zelensky for the presidency, Kolomoisky contacted Republican, former mayor of New York and Trump’s lawyer Rudy Giuliani, and actively participated in gathering compromising material about Joe Biden and his son Hunter in Ukraine. Interestingly, back in 2019, Giuliani demanded Kolomoisky’s arrest, but later, apparently, the parties switched to cooperation.

    Version 2: Biden is avenging Kolomoisky for releasing his conversation with Poroshenko. In a phone call, if we believe the published recording, between Poroshenko and Biden in 2016, the latter said: “Kolomoisky is a pain in the ass and a problem for everyone.” The publication of the conversation almost cost Biden his career. Perhaps, the pressure and arrest are personal vengeance.

    Version 3: Zelensky’s office is clearing the political field of any potential risks ahead of the 2024 elections. Zelensky still hopes to hold the presidential elections in spring 2024, but the rampant corruption is hindering the rise of his ratings. “Enough corruption!” — demand both Western allies and ordinary Ukrainians, especially those whose loved ones die on the front lines. The arrest of an oligarch who was once involved in Zelensky’s rise to power should, theoretically, ease tensions in society. Kolomoisky is a symbol of corruption and the power of big business in Ukraine. And Zelensky promised to de-oligarchize Ukraine during his presidential campaign in 2019.

    Version 5: Putin avenges the “schizophrenic”.
    In the spring of 2014, Kolomoisky, during his first press conference as the governor of the Dnipropetrovsk region, referred to Putin as a “little-statured schizophrenic who is completely inadequate and completely insane” (by the way, Kolomoisky’s height is 175 centimeters, Putin’s is 170cm). Putin, in turn, called Kolomoisky a “unique opportunist”. In Russia, the businessman is accused in a criminal case involving the use of prohibited weapons in eastern Ukraine. Kolomoisky actively opposed the “separatists” of Donbas and supported the military operation against them, even suggesting to build a wall between Russia and Ukraine. In 2014, by the decision of the Basmanny court in Moscow, Kolomoisky was arrested in absentia. Perhaps in 2020, Putin provided information about the withdrawal of funds from Privatbank to the US, and the current arrest of Kolomoisky is just another act in the slow and inexorable work of the American justice system. Next, presumably, Bogolyubov will be arrested in London.

    Version 6: SBU hides Kolomoisky from NABU.
    Not long ago, Kolomoisky was already being questioned by NABU and, rumor has it, a week later, the Bureau’s detectives were supposed to serve him with a “suspicion”. However, Zelensky, through the agencies under his control like BEZ and SBU, decided to preempt NABU patronized by Western structures and send Kolomoisky to prison first. This, on one hand, allowed Zelensky to start a major PR campaign claiming that he leads the fight against corruption. On the other hand, it will continue to keep Kolomoisky “on the hook”, increasing his dependence on the Presidential Administration, preventing NABU and the Americans from putting him on the same “hook” first, which they could use to obtain testimony from Kolomoisky regarding his connections with Zelensky and his circle. This is the version of compromat.ru and partially Sharia.

    Version 7: Global oligarchy destroys national oligarchy.
    According to Rostislav Ishchenko and Valery Pakin, representing the global oligarchic capital on the territory of Ukraine, George Soros has come into direct confrontation with the national oligarchic capital in the person of Kolomoisky. Igor Valerievich tried to negotiate and bargain, but “superpowers don’t negotiate with hucksters”. As a result, he is now being removed. In the near future, they will also remove Akhmetov, Pinchuk, and others.
